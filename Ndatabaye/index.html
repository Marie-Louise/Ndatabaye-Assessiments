AJPD-RDC

La mission d’AJPD-RDC est d’assurer un accompagnement juridique et
judiciaire aux personnes vulnérables ainsi que la promotion de la paix
et des facteurs de croissance économique des citoyens

A just World

AJPD-RDC participates in the construction of the rule of law in a Congo where
citizens live in conditions of peace favorable to their economic growth for
sustainable development.

Our beliefs

About Us

We believe in a world where each individual can enjoy their rights,
with legal and judicial support for vulnerable people as well as the promotion of peace and the factors of economic growth for citizens.

How to help us

Send your donations

Online donations are more secure than ever, convenient
and hassle-free, with just one click. We also accept
donations in cash and by check in all
of our branches.

Become a volunteer

You can get involved today by becoming a volunteer.
Sign up and you will join a group of agents of change,
a network strong enough to positively change the lives of children.

Award a scholarship

Your donation will equip children in need
with the resources, training and education
they need, while offering them the promise
of a better future. You can make a difference
today by making a commitment.
